# Welcome to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

This course introduces you to the exciting field of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. Over the next few weeks, you will gain a comprehensive understanding of how to design, develop, and deploy autonomous humanoid robots.

## Course Overview

This textbook covers a range of topics from foundational concepts in ROS 2 and robot simulation to advanced topics in NVIDIA Isaac and humanoid-specific development. You will learn about sensor integration, motion planning, human-robot interaction, and the use of large language models for conversational robotics.

## What You Will Learn

*   **Fundamentals of Physical AI**: Understand the core differences between digital and embodied intelligence.
*   **ROS 2**: Master the Robot Operating System 2 for building robust robot applications.
*   **Robot Simulation**: Utilize Gazebo and NVIDIA Isaac Sim for realistic robot simulation and testing.
*   **Humanoid Robotics**: Dive into the unique challenges and solutions for bipedal locomotion, manipulation, and balance.
*   **Conversational AI**: Integrate advanced AI models (like GPT and Whisper) for natural human-robot interaction.

## What You Will Build

By the end of this course, you will have the knowledge and skills to contribute to the development of an **autonomous humanoid robot** capable of understanding voice commands, navigating complex environments, and interacting with objects.
